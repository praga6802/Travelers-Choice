ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Arrival at Ranchi Netarhat tour starts from Ranchi, as you arrive at Ranchi airport/ railway station, you will be greeted by our representative and will be escorted to your pre-booked hotel. After reaching the hotel, check-in and start exploring the city. You will visit the Jonha Waterfalls, Dassam Waterfalls & Sita Waterfalls. After a refreshing waterfall trip, reach back to your hotel for an overnight stay as the first day of your Netarhat tour ends here..
Day 2 Ranchi to Netarhat (152.5 Kms | 3 hour 45 mins ) After having a wholesome breakfast, pack your bags and start your journey to Netarhat, known as the queen of Chotanagpur plateau. This small town is famous for its mesmerizing sunrise and sunsets. This place is less explored, which only adds to its untouched beauty of hills and green meadows. As you reach, check-in at the hotel and after freshening up, Netarhat tour package will take you to visit the famous places of the town. Visit to Netarhat Dam, Naspati Garden, Upper Ghagri and Netarhat school. Witness a beautiful sunset at the Sunset point and return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 3 Netarhat to Betla (94 Kms | 2.5 Hours) After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and drive to Betla, located in Palamu district, this village is famous for Betla national park. It will take you 2-3 hours to reach Betla and on your way you will visit Lodh waterfalls, situated in the middle of the forest and is the highest waterfall in Jharkhand. Later, visit the Suga dam and Mirchaiya falls. Netarhat tour package invites you to Betla, after reaching get checked-in at the hotel and spend the evening leisurely. Have dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 4 Betla Sightseeing & departure Wake up early for an elephant ride in Betla national park, where you will witness pangolin, four horned antelope, tiger and many other animals. It is a home to 970 species of flora. After the ride, have breakfast at the hotel or on your way and take a tour to Palamu fort, the ruins of twin forts in Palamu were once the seat of the kingdom of Cheru tribe. After the visit, have lunch and drive to Netarhat for your return journey as your Ranchi Betla Netarhat tour package completes here.
