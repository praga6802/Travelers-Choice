ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Delhi – Shimla (345kms/8hrs) On arrival, you will be received by our tour representative from New Delhi air terminal/railway station and transferred by street to Shimla. It is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and former capital of Britishers situated at an elevation of (2500 mts). You need to know that it is placed in the midst of the snow capped Shivalik mountain ranges which offer probably the most wonderful perspective of Himalaya. After your arrival, get into the hotel and refresh after a day lengthy drive. Spend the evening time for relaxation. You can enjoy your overnight stay at Shimla.
Day 2 Shimla Take your breakfast in the morning. Get ready for a tour to Shimla with our tour representatives. Visit the Naldhera Golf Course and Kufri – Snow point. In the evening, spend some time to walk through the extraordinarily much-admired spot of Shimla town which include The Mall Road & The crest. Spend your overnight stay at Shimla.
Day 3 Shimla – Manali (280kms/8hrs) After breakfast, checkout and drive to Manali – 280 kms. Manali is a flawless slope station secured with Snow Mountains & excellent waterfalls. Get back to the hotel and enjoy at the day at your leisure. Return back to the hotel at Manali for overnight stay.
Day 4 Manali On this day, you will be taken for a local tour through Manali after breakfast. The tour package includes a visit to Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Kund – Hot water spring, Tibetan Monasteries and nearby market. Enjoy the rest of the day at your leisure. Move back to hotel for overnight stay.
Day 5 Snow point Excursion After breakfast, get ready for a full day journey to Gulaba, Marhi, Kothi Gorge & Rohtang Pass (elevation 3978 meters) – is the most visited tourist spot in Himachal Pradesh round the year and Solang Valley. In the night, return back to the hotel and spend your overnight stay. (Subject to season)
Day 6 Manali – Chandigarh (320kms/8hrs) After breakfast, drive to Chandigarh. Reach the destination and check in at inn. Later, proceed for a city voyage through Chandigarh, visit Sukhna Lake, Rose enclosure, Rock garden and driver past small scale secretariat. In the night, come back to lodging and enjoy your overnight stay at Chandigarh.
Day 7 Chandigarh - Delhi (250kms/5hrs) Take your breakfast in the morning and drive to Delhi (250 Kms). On entry in Delhi, get into the hotel and relax for sometime. You can experience a great stay in Delhi.
Day 8 Delhi – Agra-Delhi (220 kms , 3.5 Hrs) Early morning after breakfast, move ahead to Agra. Visit the world popular Taj Mahal constructed by the Mughal Emperor Shahjehan in 1630 for his Queen Mumtaz Mahal to purify her mortal remains. Later, visit the Agra Fort containing the Pearl Mosque, the Halls of Public and Private Audience. Additionally, visit the Tomb of Itmad-ud-Daullah fabricated by Empress Noorjehan in memory of her father. Then, drive once more to Delhi for overnight stay. Delhi – Agra-Delhi (220 kilometers, 3.5 Hrs, One Way)
Day 9 Delhi Departure Early morning after breakfast depart from the inn and continue for half day tour to Delhi. Visit the Raj Ghat and Shanti Van- the cremation destinations of Mahatma Gandhi and Jama Masjid, Jawaharlal Nehru and drive past Red Fort and Chandni Chowk. In the evening, visit the Qutub Minar constructed by Qutub-ud-Din Aibek in the year of 1199. Drive back to Delhi airplane terminal to catch flight back home
