ITINERARY

Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Munnar Upon your arrival at Cochin, meet our tour representatives and they will take you to Munnar. On the way, visit Athirapally waterfalls. Located on the west-streaming Chalakudy River, this 24-meter (80 ft) waterfall and the close-by Vazhachal Falls are prominent visitor goals. Get into the hotel and relax for sometime. Enjoy your breakfast and get ready to explore Munnar. Visit the Mettupedi dam. This cement gravity dam is assembled between the slope reaches and it is for the most part for water storage for hydroelectric activities. Kundala Lake is a fake supply of a minor curve molded dam clustered between the mountain range.
Day 2 Munnar On this day, you will be exploring the beauty of Munnar. The surrounding perspective of Western Ghats and the valley of Theni from top station will look really stunning. Your next visit is to Echo point which is located 15kilometer from Munnar. Visit the place and shout as energetic as would be cautious you can and hold up for a moment. You can hear your echo back. It’s very interesting. If you have children with you, they will appreciate it. Continue to Rajamalai National park that is the natural habitat of the Nilgiri Tahr. Return back to hotel for night stay.
Day 3 Munnar – Thekkady After a quick breakfast, continue to Thekkady. Visit the Forest Department and explore numerous projects including Periyar Tiger Trail, Nature walk. Enjoy your overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 4 Thekkady After morning breakfast, enjoy the entire day at your leisure. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 5 Thekkady – Departure Take your morning breakfast and move to Cochin for your return.
